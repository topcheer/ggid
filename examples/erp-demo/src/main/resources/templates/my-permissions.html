How Permission Checking Works

Each action in this ERP app is protected by GGID's policy engine. When you click a button, the app calls POST /api/v1/policies/check with your user_id, resource_type, and action. GGID evaluates your roles, their permissions, and any ABAC policies to return allow/deny.

If the policy API is unreachable, a local permission matrix provides fallback.
